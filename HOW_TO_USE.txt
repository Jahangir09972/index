  HOW TO USE & DEPLOY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 1 — ADD YOUR PHOTOS
  • Put your photos inside the "images" folder
  • Open index.html in Notepad
  • Find: src="" (inside gallery-card sections)
  • Replace with: src="images/your-photo-name.jpg"
  • Example: src="images/workshop1.jpg"

STEP 2 — DEPLOY FREE ON NETLIFY
  1. Go to: https://netlify.com
  2. Sign up FREE (use Google or email)
  3. Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
  4. DRAG this entire "portfolio" FOLDER onto the page
  5. In 30 seconds you get a FREE link like:
     https://jahangir-alam.netlify.app
  6. You can share this link with anyone!

STEP 3 — CUSTOM NAME (optional, free)
  • In Netlify, click "Site configuration"
  • Change site name to: jahangiralam
  • Your link becomes: jahangiralam.netlify.app
